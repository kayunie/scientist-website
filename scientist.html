Mojo Jojo is a notorious genius chimpanzee mad scientist, whose main goal is to destroy The Powerpuff Girls, crush Townsville (or, in Powerpuff Girls Z, New Townsville), and conquer the world, in that order.
In order to do this, he spends much of his time plotting and inventing new weapons or technology. This normally comes in the shape of giant robots or large energy weapons.
He is the archenemy of Professor Utonium and the girls and the creator of the Rowdyruff Boys. Somehow, he is extremely verbose, loquacious, and communicative, so he has high-vocabulary use and long-lasting sentences, which he speaks even with nobody around to listen.
Also know as:

  1. Hobo Formerly Known as Jojo
  2. Mojo
  3. Evil Mojo Jojo
  4. Monkey Boy
  5. Bad Monkey
  6. Brainlord
